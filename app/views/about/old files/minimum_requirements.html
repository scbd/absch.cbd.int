What are the minimum requirements to use the ABS-CH?

The ABS-CH works best with the most recent internet browsers (Firefox, Chrome or Internet Explorer). Please make sure you have the most recent version of your favourite browser installed on your computer:

  • For Firefox: http://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/new/
  • For Chrome: https://www.google.com/intl/en/chrome/browser/ei
  • For Internet explorer: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-ca/internet-explorer/download-ie
If you experience problems installing a new browser verison on your computer, please try downloading "Chrome Portable" which does not require installation.
