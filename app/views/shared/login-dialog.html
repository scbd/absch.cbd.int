How do I register for a CBD account?

Any person can have a CBD account which will give you access to register information in all CBD clearing-houses including the Biosafety Clearing-House (BCH), the ABS Clearing-House (ABS-CH) and the clearing-house mechanism (CHM) under the Convention. You can register for a CBD account at accounts.cbd.int/signin

Make sure you enter all the information required as instructed on the form. You’ll automatically receive an e-mail with a link you have to click to confirm your account.

Forgot your password?

Click here accounts.cbd.int/password/reset to reset your password. You will be asked for your email address and, if you are a registered user, an email will be sent immediately to your registered email account with instructions on how to reset your password.
