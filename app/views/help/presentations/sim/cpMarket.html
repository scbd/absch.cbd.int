The Market Approval Authority CHECKPOINT

You are working at the Market Approval Authority in Wizards Kingdom, you make sure that all potions fulfill the phytosanitary and magic conditions required to ensure the quality of all potions available in the shops of the Wizards Kingdom.

The User has applied for a market approval for commercializing a new magic potion.

After checking that the application is correct and meet the phytosanitary requirements for commercialization you agree to grant the patent requested.

As an appointed checkpoint for the Nagoya Protocol, you ask the User to provide information on prior informed consent, the source of the genetic resource, the establishment of mutually agreed terms, and/or the utilization of genetic resources. You secretly hope that he will have the IRCC, as it would make your job of filling the form for the checkpoint communique easier.

The user provides you with one of the following options. YOU MUST CHOOSE:

The User provides the Internationally Recognized Certificate of Compliance (IRCC)
The user provides the national permit.
The user only tells you the magic beans in the potion come from Middle-earth

You have been tasked to do a SUBSTANTIAL EXAMINATION. You carefully look at all the information presented by the user.

* You remember to check the ABSCH to make sure you are checking the latest verison of the IRCC and that no chanages have been made to the permit.

YOU MUST CHOOSE:

Everything is in order and that you can go ahead and approve the request.
You cannot approve the request until the required conditions are met.
It seems that Harry has everything in order from your point of view. You go ahead and approve the request.
