The Provider Country: ABS Measures

As the National Focal Point and ABSCH Publishing Authority, you have been also tasked to help develop the legislation regulating the access and utilization of the magic genetic resources and associated magic knowledge and to publish it in the ABSCH.

YOU MUST CHOOSE:

You develop your ABS legislation that includes provision for mandatory prior informed consent (PIC) and approval and involvement of elf local communities whenever access to magic genetic resource or associated magic knowledge is being considered. It also includes the obligation to share benefits with the elf local communities. All USERs need to obtain PIC and establish MAT prior to any utilization of the magic genetic resources and associated magic knowledge.
Because of the difficult relationship between the centralized national government and the elf local communities, in order to save time, you develop ABS legislation without including provisions to consult or distribute benefits among the local communities when access to the magic genetic resources or associated magic knowledge are concerned. All Users need to obtain PIC and establish MAT prior to any utilization of the magic genetic resources and associated magic knowledge.
You decide not to require PIC for accessing magic genetic resources.
