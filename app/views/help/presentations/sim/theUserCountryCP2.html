The User Country: Defining Checkpoints Tasks

A summary of the information collected in the checkpoint can be sent to the ABSCH through a form called checkpoint communiqué (CPC). The ABSCH will send a copy to the designated national authority/ies within your own country and to the national focal point of the country and the competent national authority/ies of the country which is the provider of the genetic resource.

You decide to make the checkpoint/s national authorized users of the ABSCH to facilitate the task of sending the information gathered at the checkpoints to the ABSCH. You decide that as NFP you are the designated authority to receive the checkpoint communiqués sent to the ABSCH by the checkpoints in your country.

You also have to decide whether the checkpoints that you have selected in addition to sending the information to the ABSCH through the checkpoint communiqué, will do substantive examination of the information received and take measures to address situations of non-compliance. YOU MUST CHOOSE:

Task the designated checkpoints to conduct substantive examination of the information collected or received. The checkpoint needs to be sure that the user is lawfully using the magic genetic resource before allowing user request. The checkpoint is also tasked to relay information to the ABSCH via a checkpoint communiqué.
Give the checkpoint the only task of relaying available information to the ABSCH via a checkpoint communiqué.
