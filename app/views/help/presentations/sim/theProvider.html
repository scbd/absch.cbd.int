The Provider Country: Middle-earth

This is the National Focal Point and publishing authority(PA) for the ABSCH working for the National Government of the Middle-earth, in the Ministry of Environment

Middle-earth is widely known to be rich in magic genetic resources. Middle-earth’s population encompasses several independent groups of elves local communities. Magic genetic resources are important economically as well as culturally and traditionally for the Middle-earth and its local communities. Many magic genetic resources and their wild relatives are found in elves’ territories. The elves local communities are the main caretakers of Middle-earth’s magic genetic resources and the sole holders of the associated magic knowledge. These genetic resources and the ecosystems where they grow and flourish also provide many wide ranging national and global socio-economic and environmental benefits,  from contributing to the fight against climate change to supporting the basic magic needs of the most marginalized and vulnerable of Middle-earth’s population, like the Hobbits

The Provider Country's goal is:
  1. to protect the rights and well-being of the inhabitants of their country and the environment while promoting opportunities for sustainable development of the Middle-earth, and;
  2. to get the maximum monetary and non-monetary benefits from your magic genetic resources.
