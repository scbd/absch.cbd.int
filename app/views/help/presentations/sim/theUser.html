Introduction to the User

The User is a young wizard named Harry User. Harry is from Wizards Kingdom.

Harry is seeking supplies for his research on improving a magic potion based on Sopophorous Beans that has the magic power to making plants grow faster just by pouring three drops of the potion. Harry User thinks that this potion has a lot of potential for improving biodiversity in degraded areas. His research aims at modifying Sopophorous Beans to enhance its magic properties.

The User's goal is:
  1. to perfect his magic potion;
  2. do some valuable and interesting research and;
  3. to start a business selling the improved magic potion that would contribute to increasing biodiversity.
