How do I retrieve information from the ABS-CH?

To access the search tools, click on the "Search Information" button at the top of the page. The main menu, located on the left hand side, allows you to look for national records and reference records. You can use the free text entry box to look for specific titles, ABS-CH record numbers or keywords. You can also browse through specific types of documents by checking the category boxes. Both national and reference records are categorized and can be viewed following specific thematic areas, or by country/ies or regions.

In your search you can use as many filters you wish from the left hand menu.

How do I find information about a specific country?

You can view country profiles by clicking the "Browse Country Profiles" button. You can choose countries either in the map or in the list by switching the view option. Country profiles give a snapshot of all the relevant national ABS information made available to the ABS-CH on a selected country. Click on a country to view its profile.

Moreover, all types of ABS records for the country appear as a list. Click on the (+) sign to view the different records for each category. On the right of each type of record, the number of records available for that country is indicated between brackets. You can also search for specific records from that country by using the search box in the country profile.
