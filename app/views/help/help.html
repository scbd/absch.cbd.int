ABS-CH Common Formats

In the CHM common formats are standardized forms to facilitate submission of information in a harmonized manner. The ABS-CH common formats make use of controlled vocabulary and metadata in order to facilitate the consistent registering and retrieval of information in the ABS-CH. Common formats are available online in the register information page and offline in MS word format
