Notifications

In this page you will find all notifications coming from all clearing-house platforms (CBD CH, BCH and ABS-CH). The clearing-house has a system of internal notifications to deal with requests for publications, reminders to update records, or notifying that certain records might be expiring. This page will allow you to follow up on the status of your records and find what the pending requests for publications are, or when a request has been approved, recalled, or rejected in any of the clearing-house platforms.
