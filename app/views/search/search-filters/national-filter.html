National Records

National records are published by Governments and include national information relevant for the implementation of the Nagoya Protocol as well as information Parties are obliged to provide in accordance with the Protocol. National records are submitted and published by Governments and contain information that is part of a Party’s obligations under the Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety, as mandated by Article 20, and is relevant to the implementation of the Protocol.
